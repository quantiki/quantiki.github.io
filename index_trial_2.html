About the Group

We are a research group at IISER Tirupati working in theoretical condensed matter physics. Our interests span strongly correlated many‑body systems, quantum matter, quantum spin liquids, non‑equilibrium dynamics, open quantum systems, thermalisation and chaos, disordered systems, and computations and algorithms for many‑body systems.
